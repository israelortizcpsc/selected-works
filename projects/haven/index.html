Concept

Haven is a website I built for a nonprofit working in health, education, and humanitarian relief. It is designed to build donor confidence and make giving simple, with cause campaigns, programs, and clear impact metrics.

Scope

I developed Haven as a responsive, photo-led site with cause campaigns and progress tracking, program pages for healthcare, food, and clean water, a blog for field updates, and multiple ways to donate. It is hand-coded for accessibility, SEO, and fast loads to make the most of every visit.
